About

Faking It is a social deduction game. Most players get the same word, but one player is the faker who doesn't know the word. Players must describe their word without saying it, then vote to find the faker!

Why People Enjoy It

  • Quick to learn and fun to play with friends or family.
  • Encourages creativity, bluffing, and observation skills.
  • Each round is short, unpredictable, and always different.
